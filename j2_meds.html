Medicine just for you.

Tepezza

Tepezza is the first and only FDA approved drug for Thyroid Eye disease. Tepezza focuses on not only the symptoms but also the cause. This helps reduce swelling, red-eyes, and pain. Tepezza can also help to correct double vision and visual ability. A doctor's prescription is required to obtain Tepezza.

CellCept

CellCept suppress inflammation and or an overactive immune response. CellCept is a prescription medicine for people who have had a kidney, heart, or liver transplant. CellCept can help prevent rejection of the transplanted organ. After the transplant, you may take several of these anti-rejection medicines to keep your immune system from rejecting the transplant.
