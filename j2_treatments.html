Treatment Options

Eye Drops

The dry eye that can come along with thyroid eye disease makes the surface of your eye burn and feel itchy and gritty. Lubricating drops, or “artificial tears,” add moisture to the area and relieve this irritation. You’ll want to use them often, especially when you’re doing activities where you blink less, such as computer work, driving, or reading.

Tepezza

Tepezza is the first and only FDA approved drug for Thyroid Eye disease. Tepezza focuses on not only the symptoms but also the cause. This helps reduce swelling, red-eyes, and pain. Tepezza can also help to correct double vision and visual ability. A doctor's prescription is required to obtain Tepezza.

Prism Glasses

Prism correction can be used in eyeglasses for some people with double vision. This is when someone sees two separate images of one object. The prism helps align the two images, so that only one image is seen.
